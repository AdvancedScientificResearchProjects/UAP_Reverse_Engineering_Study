Focus: Chernobrov's own technical statements about time-machine work.
Introductions, general UFO/Hawking anecdotes, biographical asides, and non-technical stories omitted.

### 1.2 Operating principle — converging waves, quasi-monopole

**EN:** "Rotating physical bodies at high speeds is hard, but electromagnetic fields can be rotated at any speed — including faster than light."
