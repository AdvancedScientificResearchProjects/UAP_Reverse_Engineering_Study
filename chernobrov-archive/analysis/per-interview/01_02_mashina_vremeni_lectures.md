# Chernobrov — Machine of Time — Per-Interview Technical Digest

Bilingual extraction from two Vadim Chernobrov (Вадим Чернобров) lectures.
Focus: technical claims by Chernobrov himself about his time-machine work.
Audience banter, introductions, general UFO anecdotes, and Q&A chit-chat omitted.

Sources:
- **MUROM-14** — File 01 (`01_Chernobrov_MashinaVremeni_2h37.txt`), Muromsky lecture, mid-summer 2014 (Chernobrov explicitly says "середина лета 2014 года" at [00:02:03]). Duration 2h37m. Lecture given en route from the north to Medveditskaya Ridge.
- **LAI-14** — File 02 (`02_Chernobrov_MashinaVremeni_LAI_1h46.txt`), ЛАИ (Лаборатория Альтернативной Истории / Laboratory of Alternative History) lecture, also 2014. Duration 1h46m.

Note: Filename `01_..._2h37` is labelled "Muromsky 2025" in the task brief, but Chernobrov himself timestamps the talk as 2014 ("в настоящий момент, в середине лета 2014 года"). Both lectures are from the same year and both reference the 2001-built large apparatus as "13 years ago" at [01:37:30] (LAI) — confirming 2014 for both.
