### 2.6 Philadelphia Experiment — Chernobrov's reconstructed parameters
Lines 5988–6140. Technical figures the author attributes to declassified/press sources, passed through contacts V. Adamenko (then Soviet, later Greek):
- Young physicist **Thomas Townsend Brown** (early 1920s): invented device that "vопреки известным законам физики, за счет 'неки­ электрически­ сил' незначительно обезвешивается, всего на 1% изменять свой вес" → now called "эффект Бифельда—Брауна".
- 1925–1927: Einstein published unfinished unified field theory.
- 31 May 1943: Einstein officially enrolled in US Navy staff.
- Brown as head of division in Ship's Bureau: **$50 million** budget, dozen staff with academic degrees.
- Destroyer chosen: **DE-173 "Eldridge"**, 102 m convoy escort, keel-laid still under construction, **launched 25 July (other source: 25 June) 1943**, officially commissioned **27 August 1943** — thus «более месяца корабль был … во власти учены­» ("for more than a month the ship was in the scientists' hands").
- Principal equipment: «размагничиватели» (degaussers), claimed by Adamenko citing engineer Ed Skilling to be "очень мощный электронный прибор типа магнетрона-генератора свер­коротки­ электромагнитны­ волн" — essentially a high-power microwave magnetron.
- After the war the ship was transferred to Greece (renamed "Leon / Лев"); it was found to weigh **380 tons less** out of **1 900 tons full displacement** (≈ 20% mass reduction attributed to removed test gear).
- Valentine's explanation (quoted verbatim): by cross-coupling a coil's electric and magnetic fields (orthogonal), a third — gravitational — field might be generated by magnetic pulsation resonance.
- Reported effect: magnetic field "в форме вращающегося эллипсоида" extending 100 m around ship (varying with lunar phase and longitude); crew blurred/invisible; exterior observers saw only the crisp hull trace in water; ship enveloped in green luminous fog.
- Claimed teleportation: disappearance in Philadelphia dock → appearance near Norfolk → return "spustya несколько секунд" (≈ 350 km each way).
- Secrecy: press access to officers closed for 54 years; first veteran gathering 1999.
- Veterans cited by name: Captain Bill Van Allen (84 yr old), Mike Perlstein (72), Ray Perrino, Ed Tempany (75).
- Source citation for meeting: American Russian-language newspaper «Курьер», 1999, №65, p. 34.

## 4. Result claims (author's own stated outcomes)

- Experiments confirm «человек и Время оказывают друг на друга сильнейшее воздействие» (line 6396) — strong mutual effect between human and Time.
- Harm is not caused by motion itself but by **differential speed-of-Time across body parts** («вредное воздействие ... производит не сам процесс перемещения, а разница скорости изменения Времени на различны­ участка­ тела», line 6400).

- At sufficient Time-density differential the human eye sees the other Time as **white fog**; at still greater differential as a **glowing haze** (6407).
- Micro-level: direct link between electromagnetism, elementary-particle spin, and Time.
- Macro-level: link between rotational motion of bodies, entropy, gravity and Time.
- 1995 result: «при непосредственном участии автора на самом серьезном уровне рассматривался вопрос применения машины времени в качестве испытательного стенда, где (в перспективе) в течение нескольки­ минут можно будет проверить, как интенсивно изнашиваются ме­анизмы и стареют агрегаты при эксплуатации и ­ранении в течение десятилетий.» *"In 1995, with the author's direct participation, consideration at the most serious level was given to using the time machine as a test stand where in perspective, over several minutes, one can verify how intensively mechanisms wear and aggregates age over decades of use and storage."* (line 6524)
- Proposed seven principal applications (6445–6560): transport; piloted interstellar flight (α Centauri in 11–17 yr); instantaneous communications via gravitational waves; medical-therapeutic unshielded TM radiation; accelerated materials/aging testing (above); historical rescue / crime-filming applications; alien contact probability increase.
