## 1. Filadelfiyskiy eksperiment ("Phila / Eldridge") — TAIL OF THE ENTRY (pp. 401–406)

The chunk opens mid-entry on the **Philadelphia Experiment** (USS *Eldridge*, DE-173). This is the densest technical block in the chunk.

### Hardware claims (per Carl Lyssler / Ed Skilling / V. Adamenko, recorded by Chernobrov)
- Lyssler is described as "physicist, one of the scientists who worked in 1943 on this project". Story relayed in 1990 via Margaret Sandys (Palm Beach, Florida).
- Onboard equipment described as a **"powerful electronic device of magnetron type"** (магнетрон — generator of ultrashort electromagnetic waves, classified during WWII).
- Powered by **shipboard electric generators "of capacity sufficient to supply electric power to a small city."**
- Stated principle: very strong electromagnetic field around the ship as a screen against radar.
- Output (Lyssler version): ship disappeared; reappeared with all crew dead; **"part of their corpses had transformed into the steel — the material the ship was made of."** Hypothesis: ship sent into another time, decomposed into molecules, partial substitution of organic molecules of human bodies for metal atoms during reverse process.

### Brown / theoretical lineage
- **T. Townsend BROWN**, early 1920s: invented a device that, "contrary to known laws of physics, by means of certain electric forces" could change weight by ~1%. Effect now called **"Biefeld-Brown effect"** ("tendency of a charged capacitor to move toward the positive pole").
- **1925–1927:** Einstein published an unfinished version of his **Unified Field Theory** for electric and gravitational forces; this is presented as the theoretical underpinning — that electricity could affect gravity, "destroy or increase one's own weight."
- Brown allocated **$50 million** and "a dozen academically educated employees" as head of a department in the Naval Bureau (per Chernobrov's narration).
- 1943: Einstein and physicists already working on the atomic bomb; attitude to "Project Manhattan" among military shifted from skeptical to deeply interested. Idea of EM-field action on gravity for invisibility (via space curvature) entered the experimental queue.

### Test platform
- Ship: convoy destroyer **DE-173 "Eldridge"**, 102 m, full displacement **1900 t**.
- Sea trials: **25 July (per other data 25 June) 1943** launch; officially commissioned **27 August 1943**. ("Until 27 August the ship officially didn't exist.")
- Main experiment: **October 1943**.
- After all experiments the ship had been **lightened by 380 tonnes** (≈20% of displacement) — taken as evidence of the volume of test gear that was removed before transfer to Greece.

### Operating description (per Allende / Valentine / Muir)
- Method: "by inducing electric field in a coil, magnetic field is created; field lines mutually perpendicular. Since space has 3 components, a 3rd field — presumably **gravitational** — must exist. By suitable sequential switching of generators producing **magnetic pulsations**, this third field can be created **by the resonance principle**."
- Demagnetisers (размагничиватели) — described as **"mass" of equipment** on board, equivalent to a magnetron-type device generating ultra-short EM waves (Adamenko 1992 to author).
- Tests reportedly began on the **Delaware River** and near coast — initial declared purpose "influence of strong magnetic field on radar locator installations."
- Effect at resonance: "rotating ellipsoid" magnetic field extending **~100 m** to either side of the ship (varies "depending on position and degree of longitude"). Inside-the-field observers: "blurred outlines but could perceive each other." Outside-near observers: "saw nothing but a sharply outlined trace of the ship's hull in the water." Per Valentine: ship enveloped in "impenetrable green fog" similar to that reported in "Bermuda catastrophes." Eldridge vanished when fog completely enveloped hull.
- Reported teleportation jump "to the south of approximately **350 km** and back" (Norfolk).
- Brown himself "nearly became an invalid and immediately after the experiment went to Hawaii to be treated."

### Soviet follow-on
- Per Chernobrov: "in the 1940s **Soviet NKVD intelligence** definitively recorded the fact of the Philadelphia experiment; many of A. Einstein's experiments — not without help of intelligence — were repeated by his Russian colleague **I. Kurchatov**."
- Two ministries named as participants in subsequent Soviet work in **Severomorsk**: Ministry of Medium Machine Building (nuclear industry) and Ministry of Electronic Industry.
- **1946**: USSR Navy Commander-in-Chief received intel briefings on Philadelphia experiment.
- "In the framework of anti-SOI (anti-SDI) work an experiment **practically completely repeating Philadelphia** was conducted." US spy satellites also recorded a strange experiment with **"one of the cruisers of the Northern Fleet."** Both sides "pretended to notice nothing."
- After publication of Chernobrov, *Тайны Времени*, M.: Олимп, 1999, pp. 458–468 — first Russian book with detailed description of the project — a participant of the **Soviet variant of "Eldridge"** approached Kosmopoisk in 2000. Details remained classified at time of writing.

## 12. "ЧЕРЕП СУДЬБЫ" (Skull of Doom / Mitchell-Hedges) — pp. 421–422

- 5-kg life-size human skull from monolithic quartz; 1927 excavation by archaeologist Mitchell-Hedges, Lubaantun (British Honduras / Mayan).
- Donated 1972 to Museum of American Indians, NY.
- Specialist **F. Dorland**: skull cut from single quartz crystal **without metallic instruments**; polished using fine silicon sand + quartz dust; estimated work duration **>300 years**. [komp. вестник «когнита» No.46-00, 17/11/2000.]
- Movable lower jaw on precision-machined sockets.
- Optical analysis: built-in **prism, lens and lightguide system** — produces blinding eye-socket light from any source placed below.
- **Hewlett-Packard** mineralogy team report quoted: *"This damned thing should not exist; those who carved it had no idea of crystallography and completely ignored all axes of symmetry. I cannot imagine how it didn't shatter during manufacture."*
- 4 hypotheses listed: forgery / Egyptian-Babylon / extraterrestrial / **forgotten super-tech**: ancients knew "secret of substituting bone substance with quartz (modern science knows analogous effect — diffusion of silicon atoms into bone, but that process takes millions of years; **diffusion of quartz unknown to science even theoretically**)."
