### 1.2 Effect magnitudes — IRRECONCILABLE with earlier claims

SS part 2 gives:
- **замедление: 1,5 сек/час** (slowdown)
- **ускорение: +0,5 сек/час** (speed-up)

This is **dramatically lower** than the figures propagated in the other Chernobrov / Faraday-Lab publications. USER-RES §3 / «Эксперименты по созданию Машины Времени» / TV-99 consistently report:
- First model: «до 0,5 сек./час» (ambiguous sign)
- Later modifications: «до **40 секунд в час**»
- Ловондатр-7 human experiment 26 Aug 2001: **−3 %** over 30 min (= ≈ 54 sec/hr equivalent)
- New Energy Technologies №3, 2001 & 2003: diapason `+0,99 < t/t₀ < +1,01` (i.e. ±1 %, ≈ ±36 sec/hr)

**Discrepancy**: SS's 1,5 sec/hr is **≈ 27× smaller** than the «40 сек/час» figure Chernobrov gave in the earlier book series, and **~36× smaller** than the −3 % figure in the 2001 New Energy Technologies manned-experiment article. Two possible reconciliations (SS gives neither):
1. SS reports the **routine/repeatable** figure, while «40 сек/час» and «−3 %» were single-episode peaks.
2. SS's «1,5 сек/час» is the figure at which the effect was **stable, continuous, and direction-sign-confirmed** across all 7 installations — a conservative post-hoc consolidation. The author does not annotate which meaning applies.

Either way: **no figure on magnetic-field intensity, coil count, ЭРП layer count, frequency, power, duty cycle, or ЭРП diameter** appears in SS. The §1 paragraph is effectively a two-sentence summary with no design parameters.

- Dimensionality = **6** (aligns with Bartini's 3 space + 3 time — дата, вариант Истории, плотность/скорость Времени — as per USER-RES §1 row «Тайны Времени»).

## 3. CHRONOMIRAGE PHOTOGRAPHY / «ПАМЯТЬ ПОЛЯ» (@20,205–28,000)

### 3.1 Silanov's «Память поля» method (Voronezhgeology)

Chernobrov names **Г.М. Силанов**, geologist and head of the spectral lab of Voronezhgeologiya, as the first researcher who intentionally photographed chrono-images. Silanov's own term is **«Память поля»** ("Field Memory"). The aparatus and protocol (@20,519–22,900):

- Summer camp on the Khopra river, 200 km from Medveditskaya Gryada (char 20,519).
- **Silanov's mechanism explanation**: tectonic rift along the Khopra channel → locally elevated magnetic fluxes → «в определённом спектре фиксируются светочувствительной фотоаппаратурой» (char ~22,175).
- Exposure time reported by sceptics: **10–20 minutes** (char ~23,800) — Chernobrov's scepticism is that an exposure this long admits any passing bird/bat/animal as a «mirage» artefact.
- Success rate ≈ **one frame/year to one frame/every-two-years** (char ~26,481) — insufficient for video capture.
- Silanov himself requires an **«экстрасенс-оператор»** to obtain valid photographs (char ~25,100) — explicitly mentioned by Kosmopoisk as a **negative reproducibility indicator**: «"Космопоиск" в 1990-х годах обращался к Силанову с тем, чтобы подтвердить его блестящее открытие где-нибудь в известном месте (например, в эпицентре Тунгусского взрыва, это был бы сенсационный снимок, который всех бы убедил в правоте Силанова), но — **неудачно…**».
- Chernobrov's own verdict is cautiously sceptical: the Voronezh photographs include at least one known non-mirage defect — «пятнышко размером меньше капли, находящееся **в межкадровом пространстве**» (char ~24,900) framed by Silanov as a «сигнал», treated by newspaper photo-editors as emulsion defect.

3. «природные явления, в которых изменяется Время (**воздушных и водяных вихрей**, какими являются изгибы рек типа Жигулей и тёплое течение Гольфстрим)» (char 30,630).

Novel claim vs earlier Chernobrov books: chrono-miragery is asserted to cluster spatially near **river-meander vortices** (Жигули/Volga-bend) and the **Gulf Stream warm-current boundary**. This geographical-mechanics correlation is specific to SS.
