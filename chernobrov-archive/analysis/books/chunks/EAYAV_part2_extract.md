## 0. Source nature & scope caveat

EAYAV part 2/2 covers alphabetical entries from letter **О** (ОФТАЛЬМО…) through **Я** (ЯРИЛИНА). It is an encyclopedia of paranormal phenomena, **not an engineering monograph**. Unlike PV-01/TV-99, it contains **zero occurrences of "Ловондатр", "ЭРП", "темпоронавт", "Бартини"**, and only **1 hit for "Козырев"**. Direct Ловондатр engineering content is entirely absent.

However, the text **does** contain:
- Numerous Kosmopoisk field-expedition logs (20 occurrences of "Космопоиск") with dated coordinates and instrumentation readings;
- Chernobrov-authored original entries that coin or define terms: **ХРОНОНАЛИЗМ** (term introduced 1999, self-attributed), **СИНДРОМ ФРЕДДИ КРЮГЕРА**, **ТЕЛЕПОРТАЦИОННЫЕ МЕСТА**, **ЭКЗОАГРОНОМИЯ** (1996 Kosmopoisk neologism);
