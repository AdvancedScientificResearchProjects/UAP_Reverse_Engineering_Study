power block, measurement instruments. EM working surface (ЭРП): nested ellipsoidal layers

also affected — inversely by cube of distance. Measurement via separated quartz generators +

  / "Belgian triangle") appeared over lab; filmed by "Добрый вечер, Москва" TV crew.

  pattern in early-20th-century radio "LDE" anomalies and Theta Leonis star-map hypothesis).
