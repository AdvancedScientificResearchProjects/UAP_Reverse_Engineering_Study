## 15. ★ Камышевака / Новокубанский район — SUNFLOWER DOUBLE-KNEE CASE

- **Location:** Камышевака, Новокубанский район
- **Year:** (implied circa 2005-2008; exact year not stated)
- **Substrate:** **Подсолнечник / sunflower** — stem thickness ~2 fingers; cannot be bent manually without breaking (confirmed by Chernobrov personally).
- **★ KEY FINDING:** Two weeks after pictogram formation, stems had grown a **second knee** 20 cm above the first; both knees **indistinguishable under lab analysis** after drying.
- **Second unique feature:** Also showed burn marks (one of only 2 such cases worldwide per Chernobrov).
- **Silhouette:** 5 circles + semicircles + burn — not complex; non-public pictogram; found only because mechanizer reported it.
- **★ Technical implication:** ~10³-10⁴× apparent time acceleration during formation (reviewer-computed from seconds vs 2-week comparison).
- **Verdict:** **A** (★ PRIMARY EVIDENCE CASE — time-differential phenomenon)
- **Timestamp:** [01:39:57]-[01:43:30]
