### T1.2 — Three-level confidence protocol [NLO-DPL-24 02:12:50]

| Confidence | Prerequisites |
|------------|---------------|
| **100 %** | dense case study + exhaustive data + **original** photo/video in hand |

### T8.1 — Chernobrov's resolution [NLO-DPL-24 01:09:45–01:28:30]

1. **Many civilizations, near certainty**: "1 % of 1 %" of galaxy → millions.
2. **We are easy to find**: >100 years of radio noise; Earth **brighter than the Sun in radio band** at meter/cm wavelengths. Ostankino broadcast receivable on Moon without interference.
3. **Why no open contact?** Not a paradox — it is the rational behaviour of any civilization surveying us:
   - We kill every species we interact with in industrial quantity; **no exception** found in his search [01:20:21]–[01:25:30].
   - We kill ourselves; we weaponise every technology we acquire (nuclear physics cited in Dubna, his audience) [01:22:41].
   - Risk of arming us with their tech → too high.
4. **Non-contact is the expected equilibrium**.
