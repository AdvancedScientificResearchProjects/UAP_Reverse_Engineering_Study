## T5 — Chernobrov's "inspector's manual" — full reconstruction

Chernobrov claims authorship; published in his 2006 time-machine book. Rules he reads back:

1. **No kill** — witnesses are part of history and must stay alive
2. **No reveal** — never identify self as time-traveller; "absolutely never, 100%"
3. **Period-appropriate cover**: saints/angels pre-1900 → Martians mid-20c → aliens post-1960s
4. **Chosen-one flattery**: "we picked you out of all humanity"
5. **Mission-assignment**: tell the witness they must carry a message so they self-silence less credibly
6. **Absurd closer**: end with a logic-breaking phrase or action that will discredit the witness on retelling
7. **Memory-suppression fallback** (MIB-style) if tech is available and "humane version" allowed
8. **Run to craft** is failure — do not flee without covering

### Known counter-intel meta-goal [01:10:14]

Mock & noisify the entire topic so no rival-state scientist enters it seriously, preserving monopoly for the deploying institute's own researchers on salary.
