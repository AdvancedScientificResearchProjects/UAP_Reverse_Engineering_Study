## 0. Purpose / Назначение

**EN:** Two independent archives in this repository describe claimed reverse-engineering of UAP-associated technology. They were built in parallel, by the same methodology (ASRP v2.1), and they sit at similar evidentiary weight despite originating on opposite sides of the Cold War and framing the same physical problem from different ends. This document is the parent synthesis: convergences, divergences, and a quick-reference cross-archive table. It is not a unified theory. It is a reading aid for anyone holding both sets of claims in their head at once.

**RU:** В этом репозитории собраны два независимых архива, описывающие заявленное обратное проектирование технологий, ассоциируемых с НЛО. Они создавались параллельно, по одной и той же методологии (ASRP v2.1), и имеют сопоставимый доказательный вес, хотя возникли по разным сторонам Холодной войны и рассматривают одну и ту же физическую задачу с противоположных концов. Этот документ — родительский синтез: совпадения, расхождения и сводная перекрёстная таблица. Он не является единой теорией. Это читательский справочник для тех, кто держит оба массива заявлений в голове одновременно.

**Neither archive endorses the claims it catalogues.** Both are source-preserving research archives. Falsification evidence is quoted where it exists (Lazar's Mc isotope, Chernobrov's patent FA92 discontinuation).

## 1. The two archives at a glance / Два архива с одного взгляда

### 1.1 Bob Lazar (USA, 1988–2026)

- Post-hoc whistleblower testimony about alleged work at S-4 / Papoose (1988-12 → 1989-04).
- Public phase begins May 1989 (KLAS silhouette) and continues through 2026 (JRE #2479, S4DOC-26).
- Core thesis: nine retrieved extraterrestrial craft housed in a hangar array south of Groom Lake; one reactor studied directly; fuel is an isotope of Element 115 that generates "Gravity A" wave.
- Position relative to physics: **reverse-engineering from retrieved hardware**. Every parameter is nominally an observation of alien technology.

### 1.2 Vadim Chernobrov (USSR / Russia, 1988–2017)

- Lifelong active-research programme: MAI (Zigel group, 1982–1987) → Ловондатр-1 build (1988-04-07, first run 1988-04-08) → Kosmopoisk → Ловондатр-7 mass-trial at Medveditskaya Gryada (summer 2001) → ongoing field + experimental work until death 2017-05-18.

UAP phenomenology (landing sites, craft manoeuvres, radiation signatures) was treated as *phenomenological input*, not as hardware to copy.
- Position relative to physics: **forward engineering inspired by UAP phenomenology**. No retrieval claim. No alien-origin claim. Explicitly open-publication since 1999 (TV-99 book).

## 2. Convergences / Совпадения

The five areas below are where both archives, independently and without cross-influence visible in the corpus, land on substantively similar claims. Convergence is reported at the level of *direction and category*, not numeric equality.

### C1. Gravity-field manipulation as the core propulsion principle

**Lazar:**
- Element 115 passively generates "Gravity A" wave (carrier frequency 7.46 Hz, bandwidth ~1 µm; MASTER §2.3).
- Three gravity amplifiers below the craft focus/phase-shift that wave to create a space-time distortion that the craft then "falls into".
- 2026 reframe: repulsive force distinct from attractive gravity rather than anti-gravity cancellation (JRE2479).

**Chernobrov:**
- Nested ЭРП (electromagnetic working surfaces, 3–5 layers) generate converging spherical EM waves inside a "matryoshka" shell.
- Stated target: produce the *third* (gravitational) component of a mutually-orthogonal E ⊥ B ⊥ G field system (TV-99 p1 offset 707 020; TPV-01 §1.4; Valentine 3-orthogonal-field conjecture endorsed).
- Explicitly frames UFO manoeuvres as "temperature of the same physical problem" — time-curvature local to the craft removes the lethal-g constraint (TM-02 C3).

**Convergence verdict:** Both name **gravity as the engineered observable**, distinct from classical rocket / lift / thrust physics. Both name the underlying physics as currently unexplained by accepted GR+QFT. The physics mechanisms invoked differ (see D3).

### C2. Multidimensional spacetime framework

**Lazar:**
- Spacetime "curls" / "wraps" around the craft; the craft is not moving *through* space, it is moving space *around itself*.
- No explicit dimension count; conceptually aligned with GR-curvature-engineering.

**Chernobrov:**
- Bartini 6-dimensional world explicit: {x, y, z, t_date, t_variant, t_density} (TV-99 p1 offset 716 920; TPV-01 §2.5).
- Higher-dimensional "space-puncture" / wormhole ("прокол пространства") as telepor­tation mechanism (TV-99-p2 §2.5-2.6; Einstein-Rosen 1916 + Wheeler late-1950s cited).
- DeWitt + Graham many-worlds mapped to the t_variant axis.

**Convergence verdict:** Both treat spacetime as **more-than-4D and locally manipulable**. Chernobrov gives a specific coordinate count; Lazar does not. Convergence is directional, not quantitative.

### C3. Institutional black-program anchoring (claimed)

**Lazar:**
- Claimed recruitment to S-4 via Edward Teller (Los Alamos connection).
- Papoose Lake underground hangar facility, 9 craft, 22 personnel, 38 clearance levels above Q.
- Entire chain of custody denied by official channels. Documentary artefacts (1941 Dept-of-Interior map showing road into Papoose; 2020 Cessna aerial photo; 2024 Google Earth rectangular obfuscation patch) are the new-2026 evidentiary push.

**Chernobrov:**
- MAI Zigel group 1967–1987, open госбюджетная тема «Предварительные исследования аномальных явлений в атмосфере».
- Scientific advisor Prof. E.F. Kamenkov (MAI).
- Industrial support claimed from НПО «Салют», завод Хруничева, НПО «Энергия» (TM-02, NE-03-PG). Some of this is self-attested and only partially verified externally.
- **Crucially different from Lazar:** the institutional affiliations are *open-published*, and the group publicly-named its first press date as газета МАИ, 12 April 1991.

**Convergence verdict:** Both figures anchor their claims in named black-program-adjacent institutions. The direction converges; the **transparency of the anchoring diverges sharply** — Lazar's S-4 is denied to exist; Chernobrov's MAI/Kosmopoisk affiliations are documented and externally verifiable in ways S-4 is not. This is a convergence in *framing*, not in evidentiary weight.

### C4. Public demonstrations that went ignored

**Lazar:**
- Wednesday-night Dreamland disk-flight observations March–April 1989 (Lazar, Knapp, Goodman + others; KLAS-89b [00:40-series]). Unchallenged public witness accounts; no state acknowledgement.

**Chernobrov:**
- 18 March 1990: first-run experiment, nearby-filmed 3-star UFO configuration (file 08, «Три звёздные НЛО»; B-SVL and lectures 08, 09).
- 30 April 1991: modulated "broadcast-through-time" experiment with 5-minute acknowledgement window; 3-star UFO alleged to appear at zenith "секунда в секунду" (TV-99 p1 offset 720 400; TPV-01 §7.1).
- MAI газета «Пропеллер» first press coverage 12 April 1991; TM-02 (Техника Молодёжи, Feb 2002) second mainstream pickup.

**Convergence verdict:** Both produced **documented public demonstrations that drew media attention and then did not prompt formal scientific follow-up**. Chernobrov's broadcast-through-time case includes a filmed aerial signature; Lazar's Dreamland observations are the publicly accessible anchor for his hardware claims. In both cases the demonstrations are what the public remembers; in both cases the scientific establishment did not follow up with replication.

### C5. Time-shift phenomena adjacent to propulsion

**Lazar:**
- Secondary claim. "Missing time", time-slowing effects near an active craft, reported in passing (KLAS-89b, LT-91).
- Not the primary observable; the primary observable is lift / manoeuvre.

**Chernobrov:**
- Primary claim. The device **directly measures** t/t₀ deviations via three orthogonal methods (electronic/quartz/mechanical/atomic clocks; dual thermally-isolated quartz-oscillator beat comparison; fibre-optic pulse-delay; cross-checked against radio time signals) (TV-99 p1 offset 707 600; TPV-01 §3.4).
- Routine range: −1.5 s/h to +0.5 s/h; peak outlier −30 s/h (= −4 min / 8 h); envelope +0.99 < t/t₀ < +1.01.
- Human operator result (Ivan Konov, 2001-08-26, 19:30–20:00 MSK): reported 3% deceleration vs reference.

**Convergence verdict:** Both corpora treat **time-rate perturbation as a real observable adjacent to the gravitational propulsion effect**. The **centrality differs**: for Chernobrov, time is the primary engineered quantity; for Lazar, it is a side-effect of gravitational manipulation. The direction of agreement — that a craft of this class will drag local time relative to the outside — is non-trivial and convergent.

**Numerical reference (Chernobrov side):** see the consolidated table of 30 quantitative parameters with stability badges in [`chernobrov-archive/diagrams/numerical_params_table.svg`](./chernobrov-archive/diagrams/numerical_params_table.svg) — direct counterpart to the Lazar MASTER quantitative table; readers can place the two side-by-side for the C1 / C5 numerical comparisons (e.g. Lazar 7.46 Hz gravity-A carrier vs Chernobrov f = c/d → 43 GHz at d = 7 mm; Lazar's "missing time" qualitative vs Chernobrov's −1.5 to +0.5 s/h routine envelope).

## 3. Divergences / Расхождения

The five divergences below disqualify any naive "Lazar and Chernobrov describe the same thing" equation. They are not cosmetic. Each one traces to a different physical assumption.

### D1. Origin theory — retrieval vs first-principles

- **Lazar:** The hardware is extraterrestrial. Reverse-engineering means *measuring what aliens built*. Every parameter is, in principle, a measurement of an artefact.
- **Chernobrov:** The hardware is human-built, assembled by the author and colleagues from 1988 onward. UAP phenomenology was phenomenological *inspiration*, not hardware to copy. No retrieval claim exists anywhere in the Chernobrov corpus — not in lectures, not in books, not in articles, not in the patent.

**Consequence:** The two archives are not the same type of claim. Lazar is whistleblower testimony. Chernobrov is first-person experimental report with patent application. They cannot be cross-validated as "same observation from two sides". They are different epistemic objects that happen to converge on some parameters.

### D2. Geopolitical / institutional context

- **Lazar:** US post-WWII secret-retrieval lineage (Roswell → Aztec → Area 51 / Groom → S-4 / Papoose). Claim chain is entirely inside closed programmes.
- **Chernobrov:** Soviet / Russian indigenous research programme. Open publications from 1999 (TV-99) onward, patent application filed 2003, open Kosmopoisk field expeditions documented 1990-onward (50+ expeditions in corpus). No retrieval claim.

**Consequence:** Lazar's claims are only indirectly testable (witness corroboration, aerial photography, declassification hunt). Chernobrov's claims are *in principle* directly testable (the experimental protocols are published, the patent was public, the measurement chain is specified). **In principle** is load-bearing: no independent group has replicated the Lovondatr result in the open literature.

### D3. Technological substrate

- **Lazar:** Transuranic Element 115 ("Moscovium"), 223-neutron isotope, stable at rest, generates gravity wave passively. Proton bombardment → 116 → antihydrogen → thermionic reactor.
- **Chernobrov:** Conventional-physics components: flat electromagnets twisted into ellipsoids, nested 3–5 layer matryoshka, pulsed-mode control unit, quartz-reference measurement chain, optional magnetostrictive core (patent claim 3). Plasma envelope in larger designs. No exotic material.

**Consequence:** The substrates are **incompatible**. If Lazar is correct, Chernobrov's device would not work because it lacks the exotic gravity-source. If Chernobrov is correct, Lazar's 115 is unnecessary because pulsed EM matryoshka geometry suffices. One or both substrates must be wrong for the other to be right.

### D4. Verification record — Moscovium and patent status

This is the **most important divergence in this document**. Both positions must be reported with the same honesty.

**Lazar's Element 115 claim — falsification status:**
- Element 115 **does exist** (named Moscovium, Mc). Confirmed claim: Dubna JINR synthesised ⁴⁸Ca + ²⁴³Am → ²⁸⁸Mc in 2003–2004; IUPAC ratified name 2016.
- **Lazar's specific isotope is falsified by observation:** the actual Mc isotopes synthesised at Dubna (primarily ²⁸⁷Mc through ²⁹⁰Mc, with 172–175 neutrons) have half-lives in the **millisecond range**, not "stable at room temperature". The "island of stability" predicted around N=184 for superheavy elements has not been reached experimentally as of 2026; the nearest isotopes studied decay by α-emission within milliseconds.
- Lazar's 1989 claim of "223 g per reactor charge of a stable 115" therefore survives only if one postulates a *different* isotope (N≈184 range, or further) that has never been synthesised and whose stability is theoretically predicted but unobserved. The "stable-vs-unstable-isotope" patch (introduced by Huff on C2C-09 in 2009, adopted by Lazar in 2019 on JRE#1315) is a post-hoc reconciliation attempt, not original 1989 claim.
- Source-of-record for physics: Lazar MASTER §1.1 evolution flag explicitly notes the 1989 "flatly stable" claim was refined into "stable isotope" language *only after* Dubna's 2004 synthesis produced short-lived 115.
- ASRP-MEDIA 2021 Dubna interview: institutional JINR voice is open on SHE Factory (Nov 2020) search for stable isotopes of superheavy elements; no such stable isotope of 115 has been found.
- **Honest summary:** Element 115 existence: CORRECT (though trivially so, since stability-island predictions preceded Lazar). Lazar's specific isotope (stable at room temperature, 223 g solid fuel pellet): FALSIFIED by current experimental reach. The claim is not "proven wrong" in the strong sense — one could still wait for ²⁹⁹Mc or similar — but the claim as stated in 1989 is not supported by any synthesised isotope to 2026.

**Chernobrov's patent claim — status:**
- RU 2003110067/09A filed by A.V. Frolov + V.A. Chernobrov (2003). Published 2004-11-27.
- **Application discontinued 2005-04-21 (FA92 — withdrawal for failure to submit supplementary materials). Never granted.** (See `analysis/articles/articles_and_patent.md` §335ff.)
- Publication date 2004-11-27 only means the application text became publicly available; it does not mean the claims were reviewed and accepted.
- Rex Research English compilation of the Frolov/Chernobrov 1996 Proceedings paper is the most accessible EN text, but it reflects pre-patent positioning.
- **Honest summary:** The Lovondatr-series devices were built (multiple independent attestations in TM-02 / NE-03 / TPV-01 / KP-TEM). The *patent protection* claim is false: the patent was withdrawn administratively at the supplementary-materials stage and never granted. Separately, **no independent laboratory has replicated the Lovondatr clock-rate-shift result in the peer-reviewed literature.** The device-build claims are evidentiary-stronger than the physics claims.

**Cross-archive corroboration map (Chernobrov side):** the per-claim mapping of each Chernobrov assertion to (a) independent external events, (b) third-party attestations, and (c) falsification points is rendered in [`chernobrov-archive/diagrams/claim_corroboration.svg`](./chernobrov-archive/diagrams/claim_corroboration.svg). This is the structural counterpart to the Lazar Mc-isotope falsification record above — the two diagrams together show that **both programmes have a documented falsification register**, and neither register is empty.

### D5. Institutional status today / statements at death

- **Lazar:** Alive as of 2026-04. Still giving on-record interviews. S-4 / Papoose existence continues to be officially denied.
- **Chernobrov:** Died 2017-05-18, age 52, before any of the proposed 2020s+ device generations (I-7 projected spacecraft, etc.) could be built. The Kosmopoisk organisation continues but has not replicated the Lovondatr-7 mass-trial at Medveditskaya in the manner of 2001.

**Consequence:** The two corpora are closed asymmetrically. Lazar can still update claims (and has — the 2026 JRE2479 / S4DOC-26 cycle introduced the electret-hull, compression-resistant waveguide, and repulsive-force reframe). Chernobrov's claims are fixed at 2017 and can only be extended by archival work or third-party replication.

## 6. Methodology / Методология

Both archives follow **ASRP v2.1** standards:

1. **Bilingual pairing:** English and Russian side-by-side at every level at which a primary source is quoted. The two archives invert the primary language (Lazar EN-primary; Chernobrov RU-primary) according to their source-corpus language.
2. **Verbatim quote preservation:** Every technical claim is backed by a quoted fragment with a source-code tag (e.g., `KLAS-89b`, `LT-91`, `TV99-p1 offset 707 020`, `TPV01-p1 §1.4`, `TM-02-C10`).
3. **Source-code convention:** Each source gets a stable short code. Lazar codes: `KLAS-89a/b/c`, `LT-91`, `C2C-92/97/03/09/18`, `Rachel-93`, `UFOL2-95` ... `JRE1315`, `JRE2479`, `S4DOC-26`, `ASRP-MEDIA`. Chernobrov codes: `B-TV99`, `B-TPV01`, `B-MPN`, `B-PV01`, etc. for books; `TM-02`, `NE-03-PG`, `NE-03-FP`, `KP-EMV-OFF`, `KP-TEM`, `PAT-RF2003-GP`, `REX`, `F-12R` for articles/patent; numerical codes for lecture transcripts 01–19.
4. **Review Gates / hallucination audit:** QA_REVIEW documents in each archive re-verify sampled claims against source text with ±30s timestamp tolerance (for audio) or chunk-offset tolerance (for books).
5. **Stability badges for numerics:** ✅ STABLE · ⚠️ DRIFTED · ❌ CONTRADICTED — applied to every numerical claim in Lazar MASTER; Chernobrov archive uses CORE / NEW-2001 / CONFLICT.

## 8. Unresolved research questions / Нерешённые исследовательские вопросы

Questions where evidence is available in principle but has not been collected in either archive:

1. **Has any independent laboratory attempted to replicate the Lovondatr clock-rate-shift?** The TV-99 / TPV-01 / TM-02 / NE-03 texts specify the measurement chain in enough detail that a competent EE / metrologist could reproduce it. The open literature search for such a replication was out of scope for the Chernobrov archive Batches 1–3 and remains unanswered here.
2. **Did anyone in Dubna or elsewhere attempt to synthesise the specific Mc isotope Lazar described (N≈184 range, not the N=172–175 range synthesised 2003-2004)?** The ASRP-MEDIA interview addresses SHE Factory November 2020 capabilities; a targeted synthesis of the Lazar isotope is not documented as attempted.
3. **Is there a physical mechanism by which converging spherical EM waves can produce a gravitational third component (Chernobrov's Valentine 3-orthogonal conjecture)?** The patent application was withdrawn before substantive examination (FA92). A formal physics-review of the claim in a journal with adequate physics reviewers has not happened.
4. **If both claims were false, what explains the partial convergence in C1–C5?** Shared UFO-literature influences (Keel, Vallée, Zigel 1967 catalogue for Chernobrov; 1940s–80s cultural-memory retrieval lineage for Lazar) would produce convergent descriptions without requiring either device. This null-hypothesis explanation has not been systematically examined in either archive.
5. **Was there cross-contamination between the two corpora?** Lazar's public claims began May 1989, one year after the first Lovondatr run April 1988. Chernobrov's MAI-internal work started 1982. Explicit cross-referencing in either direction is **not found** in either corpus's transcripts or books as of this review. This absence is itself a finding.

**The largest unresolved question:** *Given that Chernobrov's device was built, operated, instrumented, and documented in open literature, and given that the measurement chain (atomic clocks, dual-isolated quartz oscillators, fibre-optic pulse-delay, radio time-signal comparison) is independently reproducible — why has no external laboratory published an attempted replication, positive or negative, in the twenty-five years since TV-99?* This is the single most important experimental question the parent archive raises. Both a successful and a null replication would substantially shift the evidentiary weight of Chernobrov's programme, and by extension, shift the weight of the C1 / C2 / C5 convergences with Lazar.
