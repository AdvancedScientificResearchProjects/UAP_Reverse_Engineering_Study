## Subject of the work / Предмет работы

## Status of the framework as physics / Статус рамки как физики

**EN:** Frolov's frameworks are **not** part of the consensus mainstream-physics curriculum. The work has been published primarily on author-personal and Russian-physics-archival sites, not in peer-reviewed journals indexed on Web of Science or Scopus. The archive does **not** assess whether the frameworks are physically correct; the archive records that the body of work is **public**, **archived**, and **citable** within its own publication infrastructure.

Работа публиковалась преимущественно на авторских и российских физических архивных сайтах, не в рецензируемых журналах, индексируемых в Web of Science или Scopus.
