**EN:** The paper reviews and summarizes approximately 20 historical government studies dating from 1933 to the present across Scandinavia, the WWII record, the United States, Canada, France, Russia, and China; covers historical private studies; surveys current scientific UAP efforts; and contains a dedicated Section 4 on UAP-nuclear-weapons correlations and a Section 5 on physical-evidence cases (Ubatuba 1957, Val Johnson 1979). The 33-author roster mixes mainstream-academic affiliations (SUNY Albany, Stanford, Wellesley, Nordita-KTH) with civilian researcher and editorial figures.
