## 1. 1989-05-15 — KLAS "Dennis" silhouette interview / KLAS интервью-силуэт "Dennis"

Interview conducted while Lazar was hidden as "Dennis." The technical content is thin; it is primarily an existence claim. All remaining material in the transcript is the 30th-anniversary retrospective narration by George Knapp/KLAS around the original clip.

Интервью записано, когда Лазар скрывался под именем "Dennis". Технического содержания мало; это преимущественно заявление о существовании. Весь остальной материал транскрипта — ретроспективный закадровый комментарий Джорджа Кнаппа/KLAS к 30-летию оригинального клипа.

### Claims about S-4 and the craft / Заявления об S-4 и аппаратах
- Nine flying saucers of extraterrestrial origin at a secret facility. Some intact and operating; others being dismantled for analysis. Lazar worked on propulsion and the power source. / Девять летающих тарелок внеземного происхождения на секретном объекте. Одни целые и работающие; другие разбираются для анализа. Лазар работал над двигательной установкой и источником энергии.
  - *"[00:01:06] Exactly what's going on up there? Well, there's several, actually nine, flying saucers, flying disks, that are out there of extraterrestrial origin, and they're basically being dismantled."*
  - *"[00:01:19] Some are in various stages of completion, built from other parts, and they're being test flown and basically just analyzed."*
  - *"[00:01:48] I was involved mainly in propulsion and the power source."*
  - *"[00:01:55] Basically, as far as I can remember, about half of them do operate, and the other half have just been torn down, basically to analyze the components to them."*
- Origin unknown to him. Information compartmentalized. / Происхождение ему неизвестно. Информация разделена по отсекам секретности.
  - *"[00:02:12] I haven't the slightest idea, and you have to understand the information is very compartmentalized."*

### Location / S-4 tests / Местоположение / испытания S-4
- Working location referred to as S-4, a facility near Papoose Lake (not Groom Lake itself). Lazar took friends out on Wednesday nights and they observed a glowing disk rise above Papoose on each of three outings. / Рабочее место — S-4, объект возле озера Papoose (не Groom Lake). Лазар возил друзей вечерами в среду, и в каждом из трёх выездов они наблюдали светящийся диск, поднимающийся над Papoose.
  - KLAS narration *"[00:03:18] They focused their attention not on Groom Lake, but in the direction of Papoose Lake, which is where Lazar said he worked, a facility he called S4."*
  - *"[00:03:27] And sure enough, on each of three outings, the groups witnessed a glowing disk rise above Papoose, even though officially there has never been a base or facility there."*

Nothing else technical is in this short silhouette clip. / Больше технического содержания в этом коротком клипе нет.

## 2. 1989-11-10 — KLAS "Best Evidence" Part 1 (exclusive long interview) / KLAS "Best Evidence" Часть 1 (эксклюзивное длинное интервью)

This is the canonical long-form 1989 interview and the densest source for the early-era technical claims. Identity revealed as Bob Lazar.

Это каноническое длинное интервью 1989 г. и самый плотный источник технических заявлений ранней эпохи. Личность раскрыта как Bob Lazar.

### Biography / credentials / Биография / квалификация
- Claims degrees in physics and electronics from MIT and Caltech (per KLAS narration; this is how KLAS introduces him — Lazar himself does not recite schools on-camera here). / Утверждает о наличии степеней по физике и электронике из MIT и Caltech (по закадровому комментарию KLAS; сам Лазар не называет вузы на камеру).
  - Narration: *"[00:02:17] With degrees in physics and electronics from MIT and Cal Tech, he has dabbled in everything from chemistry to fireworks..."*
- Jet car: 22,000-hp engine from a Navy F-3D Skyknight; over 350 mph. / Реактивный автомобиль: двигатель 22 000 л.с. от Navy F-3D Skyknight; скорость свыше 350 миль/ч.
  - *"[00:02:28] He has even assembled a jet car with a 22,000-horsepower engine salvaged from a Navy F-3D Skyknight that is capable of land speeds of over 350 miles per hour."*
- Hiring path: 1982 met Dr. Edward Teller at a Los Alamos lecture; Teller was reading a local paper (The Monitor) with a front-page article on Lazar's jet car. Sent Teller his resume, then hired by Los Alamos National Laboratory. Los Alamos base directory lists him as an employee. / Путь найма: в 1982 встретил д-ра Эдварда Теллера на лекции в Los Alamos; Теллер читал местную газету The Monitor со статьёй на первой полосе о реактивном автомобиле Лазара. Отправил Теллеру резюме, затем был нанят Los Alamos National Laboratory. В справочнике базы Los Alamos числится как сотрудник.
  - *"[00:02:44] Arriving early for a lecture at Los Alamos National Laboratory in New Mexico, given by Dr. Edward Teller…"*
  - *"[00:03:02] Dr. Teller was reading the local paper, The Monitor, with a featured front-page article on Bob…"*
  - *"[00:03:04] Later, Bob sent Dr. Teller his resume… soon after, Bob was hired by the Los Alamos National Laboratory."*
- S-4 employment: early 1989, only about six months, very infrequent basis. Senior staff physicist. Project Galileo. / Работа на S-4: начало 1989 г., около шести месяцев, на очень нерегулярной основе. Старший штатный физик. Project Galileo.
  - *"[00:03:59] … early 89, and I was probably there only about six months or so, on a very infrequent basis."*
  - *"[01:02:22] My official job description was a senior staff physicist."*
- Transport: drove to a special projects EG&G building at McCarran International Airport, parked, flew to Groom Lake, bussed to S-4. / Транспорт: ехал к спецобъекту EG&G в международном аэропорту McCarran, парковался, летел в Groom Lake, далее автобусом до S-4.
  - *"[00:04:15] At the time that I was working there at McCarran International Airport in Las Vegas, there was a special projects EG&G building… flew to Groom Lake, where I deplaned and waited for a bus, and the bus drove me down to S-4."*
- Clearance: Majestic clearance, designated as 38 levels above Q. "Q clearance is the civilian top secret clearance." 22 people total with majestic clearance for the entire project. / Допуск: Majestic, обозначен как на 38 уровней выше Q. "Q — гражданский top secret". Всего 22 человека с допуском Majestic на весь проект.
  - *"[00:10:57] No, for the entire project. There were 22 people with majestic clearance. I had majestic clearance. Majestic clearance was designated as clearance 38 levels above Q clearance. And Q clearance is the civilian top secret clearance."*

- Off the Papoose dry lakebed. Nine hangars. / У сухого дна озера Papoose. Девять ангаров.
  - *"[00:04:42] The S-4 facility is an area just off the Papoose Lakebed, Dry Lakebed, and it consists of nine hangars."*

### Propulsion & gravity / Двигательная установка и гравитация
- The craft produces a gravity wave similar to Earth's but phase-shifted so it works against the Earth's natural gravity field, producing lift. / Аппарат производит гравитационную волну, похожую на земную, но со сдвигом фазы — противодействует естественному полю Земли, создавая подъёмную силу.
  - *"[00:13:56] It produces a gravity wave, which is similar to the gravity wave that the Earth produces. However, the craft phase shifts the wave."*
- Craft generates its own gravitational field; occupants are effectively in a different "realm" while inside; inertia doesn't act on them during sharp maneuvers; gravity distorts time and space. / Аппарат генерирует собственное гравитационное поле; находящиеся внутри фактически пребывают в ином "режиме"; инерция не действует на них при резких манёврах; гравитация искажает время и пространство.
  - *"[00:14:26] Does the craft generate a gravitational field inside the craft itself? Well, the craft generates its own gravitational field."*
  - *"[00:15:02] … Well, that really wouldn't happen. Inertia would have no effect. You're in a distortion…"*
- Three gravity amplifiers. Two modes: Omicron (one generator) and Delta (all three). Delta is for space travel; craft tilts up on its side and focuses the three amplifiers on a single point. / Три гравитационных усилителя. Два режима: Omicron (один генератор) и Delta (все три). Delta — для космических перелётов; аппарат наклоняется на бок и фокусирует три усилителя в одну точку.
  - *"[00:15:25] There are three amplifiers. The craft can operate on a single one, can lift off the ground. The way in which it gets propelled are two different ways. There's what they call Omicron configuration, where the craft is using one generator, or Delta configuration, where it's utilizing all three. Delta configuration would be for space travel. Essentially, the craft will tilt up on its side… focus the three gravity generators to a single point, and move through space that way."*
- Near-Earth operation: one amplifier lifts the craft off the ground; the other two swing up in front and create a downhill distortion the craft "rolls into." Low-speed flight is unstable because Earth's gravity field varies with the mineralogy/density below it. / Работа вблизи Земли: один усилитель поднимает аппарат; два других разворачиваются вперёд и создают "спуск" — аппарат "катится" в искажение. Низкоскоростной режим нестабилен, поскольку гравитационное поле Земли меняется с минералогией/плотностью под ним.
  - *"[00:16:41] … swing the two remaining gravity generators up in front of them and create a distortion, essentially a downhill, and the craft rolls downhill for infinity."*
  - *"[00:17:13] Gravity will vary somewhat, and you will get odd movements of the craft. So its low-speed mode is kind of unstable…"*
