## 15. Overall Characterization

S4DOC-26 is essentially **a canonical-narrative restatement** with CGI. Its unique technical content is narrow: aerogel packaging, enumeration of shield-test materials, reactor-watch-seat speculation, and the "wing-clipping" interpretation of the removed amplifier. Its unique **evidentiary** content is substantial: the 1989-stamped map, the Cessna photo walkthrough, the Google Earth anomaly walkthrough (overlapping but not identical with JRE2479), and new on-camera witnesses (Joy Lazar, Mario Santa Cruz, Huff's disappearance disclosure).

The doc's narration leans on LT-91 (1991 Lazar Tape) as its script foundation. This is important for source-weighting: many striking "Lazar quotes" in S4DOC-26 are actually Lazar-voice-over reading from scripts that reproduce 1991 material — **not** independent 2026 statements.

Podcast JRE2479 contains all the substantively new 2026-era technical claims (electret, compression-resistant material, new repulsive force, stable-isotope expanded, moral reversal). The documentary contains the visual evidence and new witnesses.

Read together as a paired release, JRE2479 = Lazar's fresh 2026 physics framing; S4DOC-26 = visual/evidentiary consolidation of the canonical narrative.
