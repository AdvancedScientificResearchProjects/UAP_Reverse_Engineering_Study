## 1. Element 115

### 1.1 Basic claim: super-heavy element, fuel of the reactor
- **CONSISTENT** with 1989 story throughout. "223 gram little triangles" in triangular/wedge form.
- LK19 [00:10:33]: "223 gram little triangles of it… when it's exposed to radiation it produces its own gravitational field, its own anti-gravitational field."
- JRE2479 [01:31:37, 02:58:04]: "you need 223 grams machined into a wedge" — identical figure.

### 1.2 Stable vs unstable isotope distinction — **EXPANDED 2019, RE-EMPHASIZED 2026**
- **EXPANDED** in JRE1315 [00:27:38–29:12]: First detailed explanation that the lab-synthesized 115 (Darmstadt 2004, Moscovium, 4 atoms, 220 ms half-life) was an UNSTABLE isotope, whereas the S-4 fuel was a STABLE isotope of the same element. Analogy: hydrogen/deuterium/tritium; cesium has ~30 unstable isotopes.
- JRE2479 [01:40:44–42:25]: Re-stated more carefully — "in any element… there can be a stable version of it and a hundred or fifty different unstable elements to them." Stable 115 is physically tactile, "copper-like" color, not as dark as copper.
- LK19 [00:24:25–25:00]: Tests on the 115 — atomic absorption, x-ray fluorescence, neutron activation; "we tried to come up with other isotopes and, you know, accidentally did ourself, but it was a stable element and something we had never seen before."

### 1.3 Stable 115 came with the craft — **EXPANDED**
- JRE1315 [00:29:08]: "Did they say that the stable version had come with this craft? It absolutely came with the craft. Absolutely came with the craft." — First-ever explicit Q&A confirmation in this exact form. Matches 1989 story that 115 was already at S-4.

### 1.4 Element 115 triangular form, cyclotron interaction — **EXPANDED**
- JRE1315 [00:24:55–25:30] / JRE2479 [01:31:37, 01:43:15]: Reactor's square base behaves like a cyclotron (circular particle accelerator). X-rays show a tube/path around the 115 wedge. 115 cut at specific angle, stacked-disc form.

### 1.5 FBI raid hunted for 115 — **NEW (2019)**
- LK19 [00:10:07–10:25]: Corbell: FBI 2017 raid "looking for some of the fuel from the craft that might have been stolen… they were looking to see if he had any of that taken out of Los Alamos where they machined it."
- C2C18 discusses the raid but does not connect it directly to 115 by name.

## 4. Two Modes: Omicron / Delta

Lazar's original terminology for two propulsion modes. Not explicitly named in any of the 2018–2026 transcripts reviewed — none use the "omicron"/"delta" labels. His 2018–2026 descriptions instead distinguish:
- Lift-off hover (corona discharge, quiet, short-range) — consistent with what was historically "omicron" (local hop).
- High-performance Wednesday tests (radical maneuvers, belly-first roll) — consistent with historical "delta" mode (distortion/travel).
- JRE1315 [01:29:15] "being in its own gravitational envelope" ≈ delta-mode description without the label.

Status: terminology dropped but the concept of dual operating modes is maintained.
