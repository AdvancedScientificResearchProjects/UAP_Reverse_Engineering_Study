# Bob Lazar: Public Appearances 2018–2026

Chronological record of known interviews, podcast appearances, documentary features,
and live events focused on his S-4/Area 51 claims. Compiled April 2026.

---

## 2018

### Documentary — "Bob Lazar: Area 51 & Flying Saucers"
- **Date:** December 4, 2018 (wide digital/VOD release; festival/limited theatrical from November 2018)
- **Format:** Feature documentary, ~96 min
- **Director/Interviewer:** Jeremy Corbell; narrated by Mickey Rourke; co-produced with George Knapp
- **Where:** Netflix (added June 18, 2019), Tubi (free, current), Plex (free, current)
  - Netflix: https://www.netflix.com/title/81083891
  - Tubi: https://tubitv.com/movies/610453/bob-lazar-area-51-flying-saucers
- **Topics:** First on-camera interview Lazar had given in nearly 30 years. Covers S-4 facility
  layout, nine craft in hangars, Sport Model disc, Element 115 as fuel/power source, gravity
  amplifiers, antimatter reactor, FBI raid on United Nuclear, government surveillance and
  intimidation. Lazar reportedly made no money from the film.

### Motherboard / Vice — Written Q&A
- **Date:** December 4, 2018
- **Format:** Text article with direct quotes from Lazar
- **Outlet:** Vice / Motherboard
- **Link:** https://www.vice.com/en/article/ufo-technician-bob-lazar-speaks/
- **Topics:** Gravity propulsion, antimatter reactor, nine saucers at S-4, reasons for going
  public, concern for the American people and scientific community.

### Coast to Coast AM — "Bob Lazar & Area 51"
- **Date:** November 25, 2018
- **Format:** Radio / audio podcast, ~2 hours
- **Host:** George Noory; co-guest Jeremy Corbell
- **Link:** https://www.coasttocoastam.com/guest/lazar-bob-5916/
- **Topics:** New documentary on Lazar's involvement revealing Area 51, documentary background,
  S-4 claims overview.

## Summary Notes

**Pattern of appearances:**
- 2018–2019: Concentrated burst of activity tied to Corbell documentary and Joe Rogan JRE #1315.
- 2020–2022: Near-complete silence. COVID canceled live events. No confirmed new direct interviews.
- 2023–2024: Selective re-engagement. TMZ/Tubi special (Jan 2024) breaks the silence; archival
  material released via Disclosed UFO Files. Weaponized segment (Feb 2024).
- 2025: One recorded interview (DEBRIEFED ep. 83) kept under embargo for documentary.
- 2026: Full return driven by S4 documentary. Joe Rogan return (#2479), DEBRIEFED ep. 83,
  Weaponized Austin appearance, Camp Gagnon interview.

**Lazar's consistent technical claims across all appearances:**
- S-4 facility: ~15 miles south of Area 51 main gate at Papoose Lake, hangar doors in hillside
- Nine craft in individual hangars; Sport Model primary focus of reverse-engineering work
- Element 115 (moscovium) as fuel: produces its own gravitational and anti-gravitational field
  when bombarded by a proton, creating a gravity wave usable for propulsion
- Three gravity amplifiers on underside direct the wave; reactor produces no waste heat
- Craft hull: no seams, uniformly cold, compresses without buckling (2026: described as electret)
- Craft uses gravity wave for propulsion (bends space-time; does not travel through space)

**Notable gaps:**
- No confirmed Lex Fridman, Shawn Ryan Show, or Tucker Carlson interview found.
- 2020 and 2021 appear to be complete silence for new direct interviews.
- "It's a Very Exciting Time" podcast Episode 4 (Bob Lazar) could not be date-confirmed from
  available sources; the show's visible archive starts later than the episode's position suggests.
- The Vetted UFO Sleuth episode "Bob Lazar Reveals He Will NOT Testify Before Congress"
  (April 10, 2026, ~28 min) is a reaction/commentary episode, not a direct Lazar interview.
