# Vadim Chernobrov (Вадим Чернобров) & Kosmopoisk — Video Archive Discovery Report A

**Compiled:** April 17, 2026  
**Scope:** YouTube, RuTube, OK.ru, and cross-platform video content featuring Vadim Alexandrovich Chernobrov (1965–2017) and the Kosmopoisk research organization  
**Research method:** Multi-pass web search + direct page fetching across official Kosmopoisk channels, rideo.tv, rutube playlists, and Russian video archives

---

## Executive Summary

Vadim Chernobrov left a substantial documented video corpus. The **confirmed recoverable collection spans approximately 60–80 videos** across multiple platforms, with a concentrated core of **41 videos in the official Kosmopoisk RuTube playlist** and **~12 additional videos in a secondary "Poznaya Nepoznannoye" playlist**. Roughly 80+ video entries exist on the rideo.tv aggregation index, though many are catalog entries linking to YouTube IDs without guaranteed availability.

**Primary platforms (2026 status):**
- **RuTube channel "Kosmopoisk"** (channel ID 26423944): 106 total uploads, ~20–25 directly feature Chernobrov. Active, Russian-hosted, low deletion risk.
- **RuTube playlist 174273** ("Вадим Чернобров"): 41 curated videos, 2003–2017 span.
- **YouTube channel "UnionKOSMOPOISK"**: Confirmed active; exact video count unverified from this research pass but estimated 50–80 videos based on rideo.tv thumbnails referencing YouTube IDs.
- **OK.ru / Odnoklassniki**: Several standalone uploads including RUFORS interview and studio interview.
- **Third-party channels**: REN TV documentary segments, Roscosmos channel (last known interview, July 2017), LAI Club lectures (YouTube channel OnlineLAI).

**Highest-value videos for temporal device / experimental physics research:**
1. *Машина Времени (2016)* — primary technical content, Medveditskaya Ridge apparatus, 22 min — **Relevance: 5**
2. *Машина Времени — Time Machine (2003)* — conference documentation, spacetime engineering — **Relevance: 5**
3. *Машина Времени, ЛАИ лекция (May 15, 2014)* — full chronology of TM creation, experimental devices affecting physical time — **Relevance: 5**
4. *Исследования и экспедиции Космопоиска (2013)* — annual expedition overview including anomalous field measurements — **Relevance: 4**
5. *Хрономиражи, НЛО (2007)* — direct discussion of temporal deviation phenomena — **Relevance: 4**

**Critical gaps:**
- Pre-2003 lectures and conference recordings are largely absent or buried in unindexed archives
- REN TV appearances from the 2000s ("Странное дело" episodes, "Экстро-НЛО" 2002–2003) exist only as audio transcripts or are paywalled in TV archives
- The Roscosmos YouTube channel purged many videos in 2022 (documented by RBC: Roscosmos vs. YouTube dispute), potentially removing the July 2017 Chernobrov interview from YouTube
- Specific device demonstration footage (magnetic sphere construction, MHD generator tests) has not surfaced as standalone videos — likely embedded within longer expedition recordings

## Gaps and Limitations

### Critical Unavailable Content

1. **Pre-2003 device footage** — Chernobrov began experiments in 1987. No video from 1987–2002 prototype period has been located. The first prototype (external) and second prototype (built at RKK Energia) are described only in text sources.

2. **RKK Energia connection footage** — The second Time Machine prototype was reportedly built at the actual Russian space corporation. No video or photographic documentation of this has been located.

3. **Magnetic water / biological effect demonstrations** — The "magnetic water" effect (inward-pointing magnet sphere) claimed to affect plant growth rates is described in his books but no laboratory footage has surfaced.

4. **MHD (explosively-driven magnetic) generator tests** — The explosive-driven flux compression generator variant is mentioned in technical documentation but not found in any video.

5. **REN TV "Странное дело" appearances (2007–2012)** — Chernobrov was a regular on this popular program. These segments are in TV archives (ren.tv) but not confirmed on YouTube/RuTube in searchable form.

6. **"Экстро-НЛО" program (2002–2003)** — Three documented 14-minute segments exist only as records on kosmopoisk.org; no video URL recovered.

7. **Conference presentations 2004–2010** — Significant gap in the video record for this period.

8. **Roscosmos YouTube original** — The July 2017 Roscosmos interview (V-028) exists on RuTube but its YouTube copy may have been purged in the 2022 Roscosmos/YouTube dispute.

9. **The 41-video RuTube playlist videos 21–41** — Only the first 20 videos were retrieved in detail from playlist 174273. The remaining 21 videos are unverified; they likely include additional 2010–2017 lecture content.

### Methodological Limitations

- YouTube's search and indexing of Russian-language content is imperfect from outside Russia
- Many rideo.tv catalog entries have YouTube thumbnail IDs (~75 extracted) but could not be individually verified for availability
- The "UnionKOSMOPOISK" YouTube channel could not be directly scraped due to consent redirects; video count estimated from indirect references
